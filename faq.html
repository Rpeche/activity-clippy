Does this keep the clasp from coming undone?

Yes, this makes sure the clasp will stay in place, as the design of the clip doesn’t allow it to move from side to side.

Back to top

How easy is it to put on?

Very easy, all you do is snap it into place and slide it towards the clasp.

What colors do they come in?

Currently only in black.

Does this cover any of the sensors on the UP band?

No, it only covers your clasp from becoming undone, it does not interfere with sensors or usability of your fitness band.

Does this slide off the clasp?

No, it doesn’t slide, it stays in place.

Will these work on all UP models bands?

Activity Clippy works with the UP2, UP3, and UP4.

Do I need to glue it?

Absolutely not, you can easily take it off and clip it back onto the band.

What is it made out of?

ABS plastic, a very durable material, LEGOs are made out of the same plastic.

What if it doesn't fit on my band?

Contact us directly and we will figure out the problem together.
